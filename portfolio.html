Build Your Own Garden!

Reese and I created an interactive activity, creating your own garden! It begins with a fully drawn background (grass, flowerbed, bee), then prompts the user on the type and color of flower they want to plant. Then they watch as their garden comes to life!

Fruit Ninja

Nick and I created a super awesome and fun game that is heavily inspired by Fruit Ninja. Numerous fruits are tossed into the air and when they're clicked, they slice in half and add a point to the point total. Bombs are also tossed into the air and if they're clicked, the program stops running and the screen displays the words "Game Over."

Superhero Animal Bday Card!

Matthew and I created an interactive superhero animal birthday card! You can click on each of the animals to go to their home and once you click on the correct party item, it takes you to the party room with all the animals and fun birthday music!

Cake Simlulator

We created a cake decorating simulator on Scratch. The user creates a cake in a bakery, deciding the cake flavor, icing flavor, toppings, and candles. When the cake is finished, it takes you to a party to celebrate!
