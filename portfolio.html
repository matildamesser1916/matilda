Build Your Own Garden!

Reese and I created an interactive activity, creating your own garden! It begins with a fully drawn background (grass, flowerbed, bee), then prompts the user on the type and color of flower they want to plant. Then they watch as their garden comes to life!

Fruit Ninja

Nick and I created a super awesome and fun game that is heavily inspired by Fruit Ninja. Numerous fruits are tossed into the air and when they're clicked, they slice in half and add a point to the point total. Bombs are also tossed into the air and if they're clciked, the program stops running and the screen displays the words "Game Over."
